# How to Use Patent History - User Guide

## 🎯 Overview

Your RIT IPR Portal now saves complete patent data! You can view and download any previously analyzed patent at any time.

## 📚 What Gets Saved?

When you complete a patent analysis, everything is automatically saved:

✅ **Invention Details**
- Title, Problem, Components
- Working principle, Industry
- Unique features

✅ **AI Analysis Results**
- Innovation, Novelty, Readiness scores
- Patent type, Market potential
- Abstract, Technical description
- Patent claims, IPC codes
- Similar patents, Next steps

✅ **Applicant Information** (if you filled it)
- Your name and address
- Institution, Department
- All inventors
- Patent agent details

## 🔍 How to View a Patent from History

### Method 1: From Dashboard

1. **Login** to your account
2. You'll see **Recent Patents** section
3. Click **"👁️ View Full Details"** on any patent
4. Complete analysis opens instantly!

### Method 2: From History Page

1. Click **"📚 History"** in the sidebar
2. Browse all your patents
3. Click **"👁️ View Details"** on any patent
4. See the complete analysis

### What You Can Do After Viewing:

- ✅ Review all analysis details
- ✅ Download PDF report
- ✅ Generate IPO forms
- ✅ Edit and re-analyze
- ✅ Copy text for reference

## 📥 How to Download a Patent PDF

### Quick Download from History

1. Go to **History** page
2. Find your patent
3. Click **"📄 Download PDF"**
4. PDF downloads automatically!

### Download After Viewing

1. Click **"View Details"** on any patent
2. You're now on the results screen
3. Click **"📄 Download PDF"** in the navbar
4. Professional PDF is generated

## 📊 Understanding Your History

### Patent Cards Show:

**Top Section:**
- Patent title

- Status badge (Completed)

**Middle Section:**
- Innovation score with progress bar
- Novelty score with progress bar
- Readiness score with progress bar

**Bottom Section:**

### Color-Coded Scores:

- 🟢 **Green (70-100)**: Strong/Ready
- 🟡 **Orange (45-69)**: Moderate/Nearly Ready
- 🔴 **Red (0-44)**: Weak/Not Ready

## 🔄 Complete Workflow Example

### Scenario: Reviewing a Past Patent

1. **Login** → Dashboard opens
2. See your recent patent in the list
3. Click **"View Full Details"**
4. Review the complete analysis
5. Click **"Download PDF"** if needed
6. Or click **"Generate IPO Forms"** to proceed
7. Click **"🏠 Home"** to return to dashboard

### Scenario: Downloading Multiple Patents

1. Go to **History** page
2. Click **"Download PDF"** on Patent 1
3. Wait for download
4. Click **"Download PDF"** on Patent 2
5. Repeat for all needed patents
6. All PDFs saved to your downloads folder

## 💾 Data Storage

### Where is Data Stored?
- **Locally** in your browser cookies
- **No cloud storage** - complete privacy
- **365-day retention** - long-term access
- **Last 20 patents** - automatic cleanup

### Is My Data Safe?
- ✅ Stored only on your device
- ✅ Not sent to any server
- ✅ You control the data
- ✅ Clear cookies to delete

### What if I Clear Cookies?
- ⚠️ All history will be lost
- ⚠️ You'll need to re-analyze patents
- 💡 Download PDFs before clearing cookies

## 🚫 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't See History
**Solution:**
- Make sure you completed at least one patent analysis
- Check if cookies are enabled
- Don't use incognito/private mode

### Problem: View Button Not Working
**Solution:**
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Try logging out and back in

### Problem: PDF Not Downloading
**Solution:**
- Check browser's download settings
- Allow pop-ups for this site
- Try viewing first, then download

### Problem: Data Missing After Viewing
**Solution:**
- This shouldn't happen - all data is restored
- If it does, report the issue
- Try refreshing the page
