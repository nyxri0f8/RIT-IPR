# RIT IPR Color Scheme

## Brand Colors

### Primary Colors
- **Blue**: `#2B5F8F` (Primary blue - buttons, accents)
- **Blue Light**: `#3A7AB8` (Hover states, gradients)
- **Orange**: `#FF8A65` (Secondary accent, CTA buttons)

### Background Colors
- **White**: `#ffffff` (Main background, cards)
- **Light Gray**: `#f8f9fa` (Page background)
- **Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #ffffff 0%, #f0f4f8 50%, #e8f1f8 100%)`

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `#2c3e50` (Dark blue-gray)
- **Muted Text**: `#6c757d` (Gray for secondary text)

### UI Elements
- **Border**: `#dee2e6` (Light gray borders)
- **Success**: `#4CAF50` (Green for success states)
- **Warning**: `#FF8A65` (Orange for warnings)
- **Danger**: `#f44336` (Red for errors)

## Usage

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: Blue gradient (`#2B5F8F` to `#3A7AB8`)
- **CTA Button**: Orange gradient (`#FF6F4D` to `#FF8A65`)
- **Ghost Button**: Transparent with gray border

### Cards
- **Background**: White (`#ffffff`)
- **Border**: Light gray (`#dee2e6`)
- **Hover**: Blue border (`#2B5F8F`)
- **Selected**: Orange border (`#FF8A65`)

### Progress Indicators
- **High Score (70+)**: Green (`#4CAF50`)
- **Medium Score (45-69)**: Orange (`#FF8A65`)
- **Low Score (<45)**: Red (`#f44336`)

## Design Philosophy

The color scheme reflects RIT's professional and academic identity:
- **Blue**: Trust, technology, innovation
- **Orange**: Energy, creativity, action
- **White**: Clarity, simplicity, focus

Clean, modern, and accessible design that works well for both students and faculty.
